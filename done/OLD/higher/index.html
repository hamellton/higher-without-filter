Filling the gaps: improving the lexical and grammatical competence of C1 and higher level students

Kris Kirby

"What exactly is academic speaking? Do you exploit the whole range of tools available to us to strengthen every aspect of your students’ speaking ability in academic contexts? We’ll discuss it during my sessions."


Graham Jones

The History of Teaching Grammar in Higher Education Institutes

"'Do you know how the teaching of grammar has evolved over the last 150 years? If you'd like to know more about the Natural Approach or the Direct Method, join my session to find out more."


Yuliia Kyianets

Blended learning: interactive technologies and digital resources in teaching English

"Do you sometimes face the challenge of making your university students do their English homework at home instead of on the windowsill outside the classroom? MyEnglishLab is a perfect solution for this as well as many other challenges of teaching English. Learn more at my sessions."

Sam McKean

Developing an effective approach to enhancing the receptive and productive language skills of C1 level student

"Do your C1 students have a great understanding of the language but still struggle to speak in a natural way? Do they give up quickly when trying to infer the meaning of new words? Find out how to enhance the receptive and productive skills of higher level learners in my webinars."


Kris Kirby

The right place at the write time: leading our students to a well-structured academic essay

"Academic writing is usually quite challenging even for my stronger students. However, there are a number of life hacks that really work in my classroom. I’m ready to share them with you during my sessions."


Graham Jones

"Would you be curious to find out about my top 10 new words that have only recently appeared in English? Or discuss why English is changing so rapidly? Join my online sessions to find out."
