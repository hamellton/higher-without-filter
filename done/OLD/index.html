Welcome to the webinar programme brought to you by Dinternal Education, the exclusive partner of Pearson in Ukraine. In this series of events, focused on teaching young adult learners and those in higher education, you will discover what language and skills are assessed in the Master’s degree English entrance exam (Єдиний вступний іспит). You will also have the opportunity to thoroughly examine the best strategies and techniques for accomplishing the tasks successfully.

EVI EVENTS AREAS

EVI "Use of English"

Find out about the best practices for equipping your students with strategies that will build their confidence when approaching the Use of English tasks in EVI (Єдиний вступний іспит) and therefore ensure betters results when taking the test.

EVI "Reading"

Enhance your students’ reading skills by setting clear exam task completion algorithms that will help students effectively analyze the text while keeping them motivated and interested in the lesson itself.

The EVI Guide: tasks, tips and teaching approaches

EVI Reading part: The best ways to approach and complete multiple choice task types

When doing the reading part of any test it’s all about analyzing the texts rather than really ‘reading’ them, isn’t it? And practicing this skill is a must for any exam preparation class.
